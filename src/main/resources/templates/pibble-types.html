Pibble Types

×

Pibbles are tan/brown frenchie puppies. However, pibbles are a general classification, so all colors are considered pibbles.

×

Gmails are white frenchie puppies with black spots on their eyes.

×

Washingtons are pibbles that are being washed and can be any color. However, any black pibble is a Washington.

×

Geebles are an alien pibble race. They are usually peaceful, however will abduct pibbles at times and convert them into Geebles.

×

Bagels are golden retriever puppies. Usually light brown or blonde.

×

Footballs are frenchie puppies with a brown, white, and black coat.

×

Pebbles are adult pibbles.
